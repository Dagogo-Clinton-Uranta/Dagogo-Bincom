Mama John's Pizza

Web Design for a Pizza Outlet

Design Insights

  • This webpage is primarily a design page for an online delivery outlet and embodies a theme of black red and yellow(representing pizza colours.


  • The design consists of the following sections. A vertical navigation bar, hero section , a pizza menu section, a dessert menu section and a footer. The buttons are styled in red and turn yellow upon hover.


  • The pizza menu and dessert menu sections are identical in design, but are separated by the feature section to avoid visual repetitiveness. Please click the image/button below to view the site.
